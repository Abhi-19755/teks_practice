Prabhas

Prabhas is a popular Indian film actor known for his work in Telugu cinema. He gained nationwide fame with his iconic role as Amarendra Baahubali in the "Baahubali" series. Known for his humble nature and dedication, he is loved by fans across India. Prabhas has also appeared in Bollywood films, expanding his fan base beyond the South. His powerful screen presence and versatile acting make him a leading star in Indian cinema.
